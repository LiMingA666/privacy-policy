Explaination for permissions:

Take pictures and videos

Torch feature uses your device's camera flashlight and requires camera permission. our product will not take photos and videos by itself.

Read the contents of your USB storage

This permission allows users to view product wallpaper stored on their SD card.

Set an alarm

This permission allows users to access the device's alarm through our product clock widget.

Read Google service configuration

This information is used to acquire advertising ID. our product provides users with better advertising service by using such anonymous ID.

Change network connectivity

This permission is used in our product settings and notification toolbar, in order to change network connectivity.

Full network access

This permission is used to access the device's network for certain functions including receiving launcher update notifications or accessing app classification labels.

Run as startup

Our product will run as startup to provide users with a launcher service.

Control vibration

This permission allows our product to make the phone vibrate once after users set the device on vibrate, confirming the setting has been turned on.

Toggle sync on and off

This permission is used in our product power's save mode, and allows our product to synchronize with the user's sync settings.

Install shortcuts

This permission is used to install shortcuts on other launchers, so users can continue to use our product related functions.

Modify system settings

This permission is used in our product settings, in order to switch or adjust ringtone, vibration and brightness level of the screen.
